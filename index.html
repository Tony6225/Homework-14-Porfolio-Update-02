Introduction


Hello, and welcome to my professional portfolio. It's definitely a work in progress and I plan to continue updating as I become more skilled. Please also note that the resume linked on this page has not been updated since 2016 and my LinkedIn account is brand new.

I have a history in the field of Graphic Design, specifically Information Design. Throughout my experience as the Art Director for a marketing agency, I've designed several websites using Wordpress and supplied our development team with custom wireframes that made clients happy. Now I'm studying Full Stack Web Development at Case Western Reserve University in order to advance my career to the next level.

In today's digital environment, the online world is a busy place. Every business and service out there is constantly demanding the attention of potential customers. Proper use of interface design isn't just helpful anymore. It is absolutely necessary. If you are in need of UX d1esign services, please feel free to reach out to me using my Contact page.
